Privacy Policy for MailSense

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how MailSense collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. MailSense is an email and meeting assistant that connects to your email account and presents information in a private interface for individual users.

Important: By using MailSense, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use MailSense.

What Information We Collect

Google Account Information

When you connect MailSense to your Google account using OAuth, we may receive:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Basic profile information (display name, profile image)
  • Access tokens to read your Gmail messages

Gmail Data (Read-Only Access)

MailSense requests read-only access to your Gmail messages, which includes:

  • Email headers (sender, recipients, subject, date)
  • Email body content to analyze and display messages
  • Labels and folder information
  • Attachments (where necessary for features)

Important: MailSense does NOT request permission to send email, delete email, change Gmail settings, or manage your contacts. We only read your messages to provide the app's features.

Extracted Information

From your email data, MailSense may extract:

  • Meeting invitations (title, date, time, online meeting links)
  • Contact details from email signatures (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Reminder information from email content

Device and Diagnostic Data

  • Device type, operating system version, and region
  • App version and configuration
  • Crash logs and error reports for reliability

How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Operate MailSense: Connect to your email, sync messages, and display them in the app
  • Provide Features: Categorize messages, detect meetings and links, build contact cards
  • Communicate: Send necessary service messages and optional marketing emails (with your consent)
  • Maintain Security: Protect against misuse, debug issues, and improve performance
  • Comply with Law: Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

What We DON'T Do

  • We do NOT use your Gmail data for advertising targeting
  • We do NOT sell your Gmail data to advertisers or data brokers
  • We do NOT expose your email content to other users
  • We do NOT send, delete, or modify your emails

Data Storage and Retention

Your data may be stored:

  • Locally on your device in app storage
  • On secure servers operated by MailSense or service providers (may be in the United States and other countries)

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. When you disconnect your account, we stop accessing your Google account and delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period.

How We Share Information

MailSense does NOT sell your personal data. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Email Providers: Your data flows through Google under their own terms and policies
  • Service Providers: Hosting providers, analytics tools, and similar vendors process data to provide services to us
  • Legal Reasons: To comply with law, respond to lawful requests, or protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: If MailSense is involved in a merger or acquisition, data may be transferred with appropriate protections

Your Rights and Controls

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Ask whether we process personal data about you and receive a summary
  • Ask for correction of inaccurate information
  • Ask for deletion of personal data (subject to legal and security requirements)
  • Object to or limit certain processing, especially for direct marketing
  • Receive certain data in a portable format (where required by law)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority in your region

To exercise your rights or ask privacy questions: Email mailsense@callhomerealty.com

To revoke access: You can disconnect MailSense at any time through your Google account security settings.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including encryption in transit, access controls, and vendor security reviews. However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

You are responsible for protecting your device, keeping credentials confidential, and not sharing access codes.

Children

MailSense is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

Google API Compliance

Use and transfer of information received from Google APIs by MailSense complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide notice in the app or on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.

Email: mailsense@callhomerealty.com
