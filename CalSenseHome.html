About CalSense

CalSense is a calendar app designed for real estate professionals. It brings together your showings, closings, and client meetings from all your calendars into one unified view.

Whether you use iCloud, Google Calendar, or Outlook, CalSense reads your calendar data through iOS and presents everything you need for your day. All processing happens on your iPhone—your calendar data stays on your device.

Unified Calendar View

See all your appointments from iCloud, Google, and Outlook calendars in one place via iOS calendar accounts.

Meeting Categories

Organize appointments by type: Showing, Listing, Closing, Client Meeting, Open House, and Inspection.

One-Tap Video Join

Instantly join Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams calls with a single tap from any meeting.

Travel Time Warnings

Get alerts when back-to-back appointments leave little time for travel between locations.

Offline Access

View your schedule even without an internet connection. Your calendar data is stored locally on your device.

Privacy First

Everything happens on your device. No cloud processing or data mining by CalSense.

How It Works

  1. Add Your Calendars: Connect iCloud, Google, or Outlook calendars through iOS Settings.
  2. View Your Day: CalSense reads your calendar events and displays them in a unified dashboard.
  3. Stay Organized: Categorize meetings, join video calls instantly, and never miss an appointment.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. CalSense:

  • Accesses calendar data through iOS EventKit—no direct login to third-party services
  • Processes all data on your device—nothing is uploaded to external servers
  • Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Does not track your location

For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.
