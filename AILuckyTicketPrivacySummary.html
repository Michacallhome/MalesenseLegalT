Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 24, 2025

Overview

AI Lucky Ticket is designed to work without requiring an account. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

What We Collect

We do not ask for or collect your email address for core app use. We do not sell personal information.

What the App May Process

State Selection

You can choose your state manually. The app may store your active state selection on device so the app can remember your choice and enforce free tier state locking.

Optional Location

If you choose to allow it, the app may request approximate location to suggest your state. Location access is optional. If you deny permission, you can still select a state manually. We do not need precise location.

Diagnostics

If you enable iOS diagnostic sharing, Apple may provide aggregated crash or performance reports to help improve stability. These reports are handled through Apple system settings.

How We Use Information

We use on-device settings and any available diagnostics only to operate the app, improve stability, and troubleshoot issues.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. If we use service providers in the future, we will limit sharing to what is necessary to operate the app and we will update this policy.

Retention

On-device settings remain until you change them or delete the app. Any diagnostics shared through iOS are retained per Apple's settings and policies.

Children

AI Lucky Ticket is not intended for people below the legal lottery age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can change permissions such as location at any time in iOS Settings. You can also change your active state inside the app. In the free version, switching to a different state requires Premium.
