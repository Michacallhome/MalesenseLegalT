Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Overview

AI Lucky Ticket ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: AI Lucky Ticket does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All generated lottery numbers remain on your device only.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your generated lottery numbers
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services

Location Data

The App may request location permission solely to detect your state for showing relevant lottery games. This location data:

  • Is only accessed when you explicitly tap "Detect My Location"
  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Is not stored after determining your state

You can always manually select your state instead of using location detection.

Local Storage

The App may store your preferences (such as selected state or theme) locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Can be cleared through your device settings

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.

Children's Privacy

AI Lucky Ticket is intended for users 17 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 17.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
