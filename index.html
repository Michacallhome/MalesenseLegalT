About MailSense

MailSense is an email and meeting assistant that connects to your email account and presents information in a private interface designed for individual users. Our app helps you stay organized by highlighting important messages, detecting meetings, and extracting contact information from your email history.

Smart Email Management

View and organize email messages with intelligent categorization that highlights time-sensitive and important items.

Meeting Detection

Automatically detect meetings and online meeting links in your email content, keeping you prepared for every call.

Contact Extraction

Build contact cards based on your email history, with names, email addresses, and other details extracted from signatures.

Privacy First

Your data stays private. We use read-only Gmail access and never send, delete, or modify your messages.

How It Works

  1. Connect Your Account: Sign in with Google using secure OAuth authentication with read-only permissions.
  2. Sync Your Email: MailSense reads your messages and metadata to organize and display them in the app.
  3. Stay Organized: View categorized emails, upcoming meetings, and extracted contact information all in one place.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. MailSense:

  • Requests only read-only access to your Gmail messages
  • Does not request permission to send, delete, or modify emails
  • Does not use your Gmail data for advertising
  • Does not sell your data to third parties
  • Complies with Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements

For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.

How MailSense Uses Your Google Data

MailSense connects to your Gmail account through Google Sign-In using secure OAuth authentication. Here is exactly what we access and how we use it:

What Gmail Data We Access

  • Message metadata: Sender, recipients, subject lines, and dates to organize and prioritize your emails
  • Message body content: Email text is parsed on your device to detect meetings, extract contact information, and identify important items
  • Labels and folders: To display your email organization within the app

Our Access Is Read-Only

MailSense uses the gmail.readonly scope. This means:

  • We cannot send emails on your behalf
  • We cannot delete or modify your messages
  • We cannot change your Gmail settings
  • We can only read your messages to provide the app's features

All Processing Happens On Your Device

Your email content is processed entirely on your iPhone. MailSense does not transmit, store, or process your email content on any external servers. Your emails never leave your phone.

What We Surface For You

  • Meetings with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams links
  • Important messages that need your attention
  • Contact information extracted from email signatures
  • Reminders and action items from your email content
