Privacy Policy for MailSense

Last updated: December 1, 2025

1. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MailSense and the MailSense operator collect, use, and share information when you:

  • use the MailSense mobile application
  • visit the MailSense legal and support pages that the operator makes available for the app

This Policy does not apply to any other websites or services that may be linked from these pages. Those services have their own policies.

MailSense is an e mail and meeting assistant that connects to your e mail account and presents information in a private interface for individual users.

By installing or using MailSense, or by visiting the MailSense legal or support pages, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you should not use MailSense.

2. Controller and contact details

For personal data covered by this Policy, the MailSense operator acts as the data controller.

Contact for privacy questions or requests

Email: mailsense at callhomerealty dot com

The legal name, address, and registration details of the MailSense operator are maintained in the operator records and can be added by counsel if needed.

3. Information we collect

MailSense can access and process several categories of information. Some information is provided directly by you and some is obtained through your e mail provider when you connect the app.

3.1 Information from Google sign in

When you connect MailSense to your Google account, MailSense may receive:

  • your Google account e mail address
  • basic profile information such as display name and profile image
  • technical identifiers and tokens that allow MailSense to access the Gmail application programming interface on your behalf

3A. Google account access

MailSense connects to your Google account using Google OAuth. When you choose to connect, MailSense requests only the permissions needed to provide its features, which currently include:

  • basic Google profile information such as your name and e mail address
  • read only access to your Gmail messages and related metadata

MailSense does not request permission to send e mail, delete e mail, change Gmail settings, or manage your contacts.

You can choose at any time not to connect a Google account. If you do not connect a Google account, MailSense cannot read any of your Gmail data.

3.2 E mail content and metadata

Through the permissions you grant, MailSense may access:

  • e mail headers such as sender, recipients, subject, date, and message identifiers
  • e mail body content in order to analyze and display messages in the app
  • label and folder information that helps organize messages
  • attachments, but only where necessary to support visible features in the app

MailSense uses Gmail data only in order to provide the features described in this Policy. MailSense uses Gmail data in accordance with the Google application programming interface Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

MailSense reads messages and metadata but does not send messages, delete messages, or modify labels or folders in your Gmail account.

3.3 Meeting and reminder information

From your e mail data, MailSense may extract:

  • meeting invitations and calendar style details such as title, date, time, and online meeting links
  • reminder style information that appears in e mail content
  • related context such as who is invited and what the meeting is about

3.4 Contact details

From your e mail interactions and signatures, MailSense may extract:

  • names, e mail addresses, and phone numbers
  • job titles, company names, and websites
  • other contact information included in signature blocks

These data are used to show and manage your own contact views in the app. MailSense does not create a public contact directory.

3.5 Device and diagnostic data

When you use the app, MailSense may collect:

  • device type, operating system version, language settings, and region
  • app version and configuration data
  • diagnostic information such as crash logs, error reports, and basic analytics for performance and reliability

Third party tools may provide some of these diagnostics on behalf of the operator.

3.6 Communication preferences

If you choose to receive product tips or news, MailSense may collect:

  • your e mail address and subscription status
  • records that a message was sent and whether you opened or unsubscribed

4. How we use information

The operator processes the information described above for the following purposes.

To operate MailSense

  • connect to your e mail account through Google
  • sync and display your messages and related data in the app
  • extract meetings, links, and contact details and present them in organized views

To provide app features and analysis

  • categorize messages by type or importance
  • parse dates and times for meetings and reminders
  • build contact cards based on your own e mail history

To communicate with you

  • send necessary service messages such as changes to this Policy, security alerts, or account notices
  • send marketing e mails only where you have clearly consented, with a simple way to opt out at any time

To maintain security and reliability

  • protect the app and operator systems from misuse, fraud, or abuse
  • debug, monitor, and improve performance

To comply with legal obligations

  • keep records that the operator is required to maintain by law
  • respond to lawful requests from public authorities where applicable

MailSense does not provide a social network and does not expose your e mail content to other users.

MailSense does not use Gmail data for advertising targeting and does not sell Gmail data to advertisers or data brokers.

Human access to Gmail data is limited to cases where it is necessary for security reasons, to comply with law, or to investigate and fix specific problems at your request.

5. Data storage, location, and retention

5.1 Storage locations

Data used by MailSense may be stored:

  • locally on your device in app storage
  • on secure servers operated by the MailSense operator or its service providers, which may be located in the United States and other countries where those providers maintain facilities

The exact mix of device processing and server processing may change as the app evolves. The MailSense operator will use commercially reasonable measures to keep your personal data protected wherever it is stored.

5.2 Retention

The operator keeps personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law. In general:

  • e mail content and related data are retained while your MailSense connection to that e mail account remains active
  • contact and meeting extracts are retained while needed to support app features you use
  • diagnostic and analytics data are kept for a limited period that allows performance analysis and maintenance of security
  • backup copies may remain for a limited time as part of routine backup procedures

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the legal obligations that apply.

5.3 Disconnection and deletion

You may request that the operator disconnect your MailSense integration or remove stored data by contacting mailsense at callhomerealty dot com.

When you disconnect:

  • the operator will stop using the tokens that allow MailSense to access your Google account
  • the operator will delete or anonymize personal data from active systems within a reasonable period, except where it is legally required or reasonably permitted to keep some data, for example for accounting, security logs, or backup integrity

You can also revoke MailSense access at any time directly in your Google account security settings.

6. How we share information

MailSense does not sell your personal data and does not share your Gmail content with advertisers.

The operator may share information as follows.

E mail and platform providers

Your e mail data flows through your provider, such as Google, under their own terms and policies.

Service providers working for the operator

  • hosting providers, analytics tools, e mail delivery services, and similar vendors may process data so they can provide services to the operator
  • these service providers are required to use the data only to provide services to the MailSense operator and to protect the data appropriately

Legal and safety reasons

The operator may disclose information if it believes this is reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect rights or property, or protect users or the public.

Business transfers

If the MailSense operator is involved in a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality controls.

7. Marketing and communication choices

MailSense distinguishes between:

  • service communication, meaning messages about security, account changes, and important updates that are necessary for the app
  • marketing communication, meaning optional messages such as tips, feature announcements, and upgrade offers

Marketing e mails are sent only where allowed by law and only if you have given clear consent or where the law otherwise permits.

You can opt out of marketing e mails at any time by using the unsubscribe link in those messages or by e mailing mailsense at callhomerealty dot com. Opting out of marketing does not stop service messages and does not remove access to any existing plan.

8. User rights and controls

The rights that apply to your personal data depend on where you live and which privacy laws apply to the MailSense operator and to you.

The operator will handle requests in a reasonable way and within the limits of applicable law. In some cases, a request may be refused or only partly granted.

Subject to applicable law, you may be able to:

  • ask whether the operator processes personal data about you and, where required, receive a summary of that data
  • ask for correction of inaccurate information that you cannot correct yourself
  • ask for deletion of personal data, subject to the need to keep certain information for legal, security, or business reasons
  • ask the operator to limit or object to some processing, especially for direct marketing
  • ask, where required by law and technically feasible, to receive certain data in a structured, commonly used format

You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority or similar regulator in your region if you believe your rights have been violated.

To contact the operator about privacy rights, e mail mailsense at callhomerealty dot com with a clear description of your request. The operator may ask for additional information to confirm your identity and to clarify what you are asking it to do.

The operator may decline or limit your request where law allows or requires that result, including where a request is manifestly unfounded or excessive or would adversely affect the rights of another person.

9. Security

The MailSense operator uses reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect personal data against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

These measures may include encryption in transit, access controls, and vendor security reviews. No system can be guaranteed completely secure.

You are responsible for:

  • protecting your device with appropriate security settings
  • keeping your e mail and device credentials confidential
  • not sharing codes or passwords that provide access to your MailSense connection

If you believe your MailSense integration has been misused, contact mailsense at callhomerealty dot com.

10. Children

MailSense is designed for adult users and older teenagers who are allowed to manage their own e mail accounts.

MailSense is not directed to children under thirteen years of age, and the operator does not knowingly collect personal data from children under thirteen. If the operator learns that data were collected from a child under thirteen, it will take reasonable steps to delete that data and close any associated access.

Parents or guardians who believe a child under thirteen has used MailSense may contact mailsense at callhomerealty dot com.

11. Changes to this Policy

The MailSense operator may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes are material, a notice will be provided in the app or on the legal pages with a new Last updated date. Your continued use of MailSense after the effective date of any change means you accept the updated Policy.

12. Compliance with Google policies

Use and transfer of information received from Google application programming interfaces by MailSense will comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
