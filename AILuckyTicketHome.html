AI Lucky Ticket

AI Lucky Ticket is an iOS entertainment app that generates lottery number combinations and shows optional pattern-style views to keep picks fun and varied.

Important Notice

Lottery outcomes are random and independent. Past results do not predict future results. This app cannot predict outcomes and cannot improve your odds of winning.

What You Can Do

  • Generate number combinations for supported lottery games
  • Choose your state manually, or optionally allow approximate location to suggest a state
  • In the free version, your active state is locked after selection. Other states remain locked until Premium is purchased
  • View historical lottery statistics for informational interest only

About the Historical Stats

For trivia and curiosity, the app may display historical statistics compiled from about 10 years of publicly available past winning results. These statistics are shown for informational interest only and have no bearing on future drawings.

No Ticket Purchase

AI Lucky Ticket does not sell lottery tickets, does not place bets, and does not submit entries. Always follow your official local lottery rules.
