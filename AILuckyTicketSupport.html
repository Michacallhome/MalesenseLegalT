Frequently Asked Questions

Does AI Lucky Ticket predict winning lottery numbers?

No. AI Lucky Ticket is an entertainment app that generates number combinations using algorithms. Lottery games are games of pure chance, and no app or system can predict or influence the outcome. The numbers we generate are for fun and entertainment only.

How do the "hot" and "cold" numbers work?

Hot and cold numbers are based on historical frequency data - how often certain numbers have appeared in past drawings. While interesting to view, past frequency has no bearing on future drawings. Each lottery draw is an independent random event.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free users can generate a limited number of tickets per draw window. Premium subscribers get unlimited generations, access to all lottery games, and additional customization options.

How do I restore my subscription?

If you've previously subscribed and need to restore your purchase, go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Why do you need my location?

Location is optional and only used to automatically select your state for relevant lottery games. You can always manually select your state instead. Location data is processed on-device and never sent to our servers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. To cancel, go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find AI Lucky Ticket, and tap Cancel Subscription.
