Quick Troubleshooting

If the app shows a blank screen or an error:

  • Close the app and reopen it
  • Confirm you are on the latest App Store version
  • Restart your device if needed

State selection and location:

You can always choose your state manually. Location is optional and only used to suggest a state. In the free version, once you select an active state, other states remain locked unless Premium is purchased.

Common Questions

Does the app increase my odds or guarantee a win?

No. Lottery outcomes are random and independent. The app is for entertainment only and cannot improve odds.

Does the app collect my email?

No. The app is designed to work without an account and does not require email for core use.

Why do you show historical stats?

For informational interest only. Past results do not predict future outcomes.

How do I switch states?

In the free version, you can select your active state but other states remain locked unless Premium is purchased. Premium allows switching and access to more states.
